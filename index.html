About

I am miqdad23, a student with too many tabs open — physics notes, color grades, half-written captions. I collect light from Manikganj’s fields, Dhaka’s traffic, and all the quiet corners in between. Sometimes it’s a dog on a sleepy road, sometimes it’s rain on an empty rooftop. I press record on the small, fragile things the world is too busy to notice, and turn them into frames, timelines, and tiny universes you can scroll through.

This is my way of remembering that even a random evening can look cinematic if you look long enough.
